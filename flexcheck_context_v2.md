# FlexCheck — context.md v2

## What is FlexCheck?

FlexCheck is a dark, premium web app that lets users generate realistic AI photos and videos of themselves in luxury lifestyle scenarios. The user uploads a photo, describes what they want ("Put me in a Ferrari 488 GTB cockpit, red interior, night city view"), and the AI generates a realistic photo or animated video in under 60 seconds — ready to post on Instagram Stories or Snapchat.

**One-line pitch:** "Your life. Upgraded."

**Target audience:** US Gen Z and millennials aged 16-30 who create lifestyle content for social media.

**Business model:** Credit-based subscription + credit packs.

See README.md in this project for setup instructions.
